## Business Understanding
The IPL is one of the world's most valuable sports leagues. Teams, coaches,
and analysts use data to make decisions on player auctions, match strategy,
and franchise performance. This project uses 17 years of IPL data to answer:

- Which franchises dominate and why?
- Does winning the toss actually help you win the match?
- Who are the most impactful batsmen and bowlers in IPL history?
- How has scoring and match intensity changed over the seasons?

---

## About
Interactive Tableau dashboards analyzing **17 seasons of IPL cricket (2008–2024)**
using 1,095 matches and 260,920 ball-by-ball deliveries.

## Business Insights Explained

**🏆 Mumbai Indians — Built to Win**
With 144 wins, MI's dominance reflects smart auction planning, strong
leadership under Rohit Sharma, and a consistent core squad strategy.
Their model is a benchmark for franchise management in T20 cricket.

**🪙 Toss = Field First**
Teams winning the toss and fielding first won at 34.43% — more than double
the 16.16% win rate when batting first. This tells coaches that chasing
a target is a strategic advantage in T20 cricket, as teams can pace
their innings with a clear goal in sight.

**📈 The Game is Getting Bigger**
Runs per season grew from 16,353 in 2008 to 25,971 in 2024. Better
pitches, flatter surfaces, stronger batting depth, and power-hitting
techniques have transformed IPL into a high-scoring spectacle — which
directly impacts broadcast value and audience engagement.

**🏏 Kohli's Consistency = Auction Gold**
Virat Kohli's 8,014 runs across 17 seasons shows unmatched longevity.
For franchise owners, a player with this consistency justifies a premium
auction bid — reducing risk while guaranteeing run contributions.

**🎳 Wrist Spin Wins**
YS Chahal (213), PP Chawla (201), and SP Narine dominating the wickets
list proves leg-spin and mystery spin are the most effective bowling
strategies in T20. Teams that invest in quality spinners consistently
outperform those relying purely on pace.
